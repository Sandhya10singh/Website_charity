Test Assignments for Volunteers & Interns

  • Graphic Designer Test assignment

    Dear Applicant, We thank you for showing interest in our internship/Volunteer program. Your application is shortlisted for the post of Graphic Designer and now the next step is test assignment. So for the task of Graphic Designer as a test you need to make a 500*500 pixel poster. You need to make a poster based on the content available in this link : https://dreamgirlfoundation.ngo/member-for-icc . This poster will be used on the social media platforms offering our representative for ICC of other companies. You have freedom to choose the colours, making little bit adjustments in the logo and the name. Font style shall be as per your choice. But it should be clearly visible. The required information is as follows : Name : Dream Girl Foundation Slogan : We are changing thousands of dreams into reality. Logo : provided in attachment Phone No. : 9873133134 The poster should be suitable & looks good on the social media platforms. You can use images from our website or from our fb page. You need to submit this poster within 2 days. What We Are Offering: You will be awarded with a certificate of Internship/Volunteer for working as the Graphics Designer in our organisation after completion of 2 months only. This Internship/Volunteer opportunity will be unpaid. Thanks & Regards, Team Dream Girl Foundation

  • Social Media Test Assignment

    We thank you for showing your interest in working with us as a Social Media Manager. We have a small test assignment for you. You need to make a Social Media Post with an image or GIF related to our cause. The content and image should be related to the current trend of Social media (Not more than 10 days old) and should be related to our organisation. It means you can design your post related to Volunteer, Interns, Fundraising, Donation-in Kind or any near festival celebration. For content you may visit our website https://dreamgirlfoundation.ngo as well. Your post should be engaging and witty to get the attention of visitors. For submitting the test assignment you need to post it from your own anyone Social media channel i.e. Facebook, Instagram or LinkedIn. You need to tag Dream Girl Foundation Social media channel along with hashtag #SocialMediaManager with any other hashtags related to Social Media posts. Finding an image and making it to the size of a Social Media handle is your task. This assignment needs to be submitted within 2 days and it is an unpaid opportunity. For any query you may email us Regards, Team Dream Girl Foundation

  • Content Writer Test Assignment

    Dear Applicant, We thank you for showing your interest in working with us as a content writer. This opportunity is available for a period starting from 15th April. We have a small test assignment for you. You need to convert a fundraising story in a success story. A Fundraising story is a story of a child who needs financial support for her/his treatment and success story is the same story but thanking Donor for their support and informing about successful surgery of the child. You can read some example of success story on https://dreamgirlfoundation.ngo/recent-cases/ You can take a story from this page : https://pages.razorpay.com/pl_F646D6mu9acEhX/view and convert it into success story You need to make it in a way that it should attach the reader emotionally and Donor can realize what its contribution has done. Regards, Ankit Dream Girl Foundation

  • FundRaiser Next Step

    Dear Candidate, Greetings from Dream Girl Foundation, Thank you for your email. We always appreciate young talent and are in favour of giving them a chance to grow in the Social Sector. It will be our pleasure to have an association with you. For becoming digital fundraisers we have created a platform where you can register as a fundraiser for our organization online within minutes and can also track on a real time basis how much amount you have raised. For that you need to visit https://fundraisers.giveindia.org/nonprofits/dream-girl-foundation/ and click on the button “Start a Fundraiser” and register. And that’s it. You are ready to raise funds with your own custom link for our organization. Do let us know if you have any questions or concerns through email on info@dreamgirlfoundation.ngo. In addition to this we also provide certificates to Fundraisers who raise a minimum of Rs. 10,000 for our organization. After Completion of target, do send an email on info@dreamgirfloundation.ngo for certificate. Regards Team Dream Girl Foundation
